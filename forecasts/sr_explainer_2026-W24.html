1 · The method — two layers

Raw levels come from code (fractal pivots). They only become tradeable S/R zones after passing the Z1 qualifier rules. Confluence (Z2–Z7) does not draw the box — it only scores an already-located zone.

1
Fractal pivot detection (find_pivots, N=2). A bar is a pivot HIGH if its high is strictly greater than the highs of the 2 bars on each side; pivot LOW symmetric on low. Needs 2 confirming bars right → newest pivot lags ~2 bars (intended).
2
Swing scan at N=5 for the published table (wider half-width = only major turns). Daily on raw bars, H4 on trading-day bars only.
3
Weekly pivots computed from prior week OHLC — pure arithmetic, not fractals (see formula below).
4
Z1 qualify → zone. A pivot/pivot-cluster becomes valid S/R only if D1/W1 AND one of: prior swing H/L with 2+ reactions · role-reversal retest · round# at structure. Dedup: two levels at same price = ONE zone.
Weekly pivot formula (prior-week OHLC): PP = (H + L + C) / 3 R1 = 2·PP − L S1 = 2·PP − H R2 = PP + (H−L) S2 = PP − (H−L) R3 = H + 2·(PP−L) S3 = L − 2·(H−PP)
